# The Great Discovery
### Roadmap

*This is not a feature list. It is a description of constraints narrowing — each phase ruling out more of what the engine cannot yet be, until what it must be settles into place.*

---

## How To Read This Roadmap

Features are added to products.
Constraints are discovered by engines.

This roadmap is not a promise of additions. It is a map of the settling process — each phase leaving the engine more structurally opinionated, more precise in its holes, closer to the configuration that the whole project implies but cannot yet fully occupy.

Nothing here will be forced.
If something won't settle, that is signal, not failure.

---

## Phase 0 — Structural Foundation
*Status: Complete (current state)*

The engine exists. It grows a graph, measures pressure, detects forbidden motifs, and accumulates the edges of the table. The loop is closed. The dynamics are real.

What is true at this phase:
- Nodes are anonymous — pure topology, no meaning
- Motifs are detected but not isomorphism-invariant
- Hole allocation is transitive closure (logic, not physics)
- Exploration is unbiased — no pull toward holes
- Forbidden motifs accumulate but do not yet actively shape exploration

What this phase proved:
The pressure dynamics work. Compression spikes are real signal. The feedback loop is genuine. The table can be built. The engine is not a concept — it is a running system.

---

## Phase 1 — Structural Integrity
*Validate and harden the foundation before adding meaning*

The goal of this phase is not new capability. It is making sure the existing capability is correct.

**Isomorphism-invariant signatures**
Two structurally identical subgraphs must produce the same motif signature regardless of node identity. This is the difference between a vocabulary of shapes and a vocabulary of accidents. Requires canonical graph labeling — practical approximations exist.

**Scalable pressure measurement**
Replace full combinatorial sampling with importance-weighted sampling biased toward structurally significant regions. The engine must remain useful as the graph grows to thousands of nodes.

**Governance calibration**
The forbidden threshold (currently 0.15) must be tuned against real graph behavior. Too sensitive and noise pollutes the table. Too coarse and real edges are missed. This requires running the engine long enough to develop a baseline.

**Bias exploration toward holes**
New node placement should not be random. It should be drawn toward structurally underrepresented regions — toward where the pressure is highest and the holes are most precisely shaped. This is the first step toward exploration that knows where it is going.

*This phase is complete when the structural dynamics are trustworthy enough to load meaning into.*

---

## Phase 2 — The Abstraction Layer
*Introduce meaning without forcing it*

This is the most delicate phase. Meaning must be introduced in a way that the structure can accept — not hammered in, not glued down, but offered to the topology and allowed to settle.

**Typed nodes**
Nodes acquire meaning anchors: concepts, domains, questions, archetypes. These are not definitions — they are orientations. A node anchored to "causality" is not a database entry for causality. It is a point in the topology that the concept of causality is drawn toward.

**Typed edges**
Edges acquire relationship types: *causes, contradicts, requires, emerges from, is a kind of, is forbidden by.* Typed edges change the motif vocabulary — the same structural pattern with different edge types becomes a different motif. The table becomes richer.

**Semantic pressure**
Compression and entropy are measured not just structurally but semantically. Do the concepts clustering together in the topology actually belong together? Does the semantic weight of a region match its structural weight? Mismatches are signal.

**True settling — energy minimization**
Hole allocation is rebuilt from transitive closure into genuine energy minimization. The hole exerts a structural demand. The surrounding forbidden motifs define what cannot fill it. The candidate concepts are evaluated not by logic but by fit — does this concept reduce the system's pressure or increase it? What settles is what was always going to settle.

*This phase is complete when the engine can say, in structural terms, "something belongs here, and it is shaped like this."*

---

## Phase 3 — Named Holes
*The engine begins to ask questions*

When a hole's shape becomes precise enough — when the surrounding constraints have narrowed it to the point where only a very specific kind of thing can fill it — the engine surfaces that hole in language.

Not as an answer. As a question.

"There is a concept that causality requires and contradiction forbids, that emerges from symmetry and is required by the structure between these seven nodes. It does not yet have a name. Here is its shape."

This is the moment the engine stops being a mapper and starts being a **questioner** — generating not knowledge but the precise outlines of what knowledge must exist.

These named holes are the engine's primary output at maturity. They are the load-bearing absences made visible. They are Mendeleev pointing at the gap and saying: something must go here, and it will have these properties, and when you find it, you will recognize it immediately.

*This phase is complete when the engine produces a named hole that nobody asked it to find, in a domain nobody told it to explore, that turns out to be real.*

---

## Phase 4 — Recursive Sharpening
*The mapmaker becomes the map*

At this phase, the engine's output — the named holes, the forbidden motifs, the settled configurations — is fed back into the engine as input. The map becomes a node. The act of discovery becomes part of the topology being discovered.

This is the recursive heart that the project was always pointing toward.

The engine is no longer just mapping knowledge.
It is mapping the process of its own mapping.
And in doing so, it sharpens both.

*This phase has no completion condition. It is the engine doing what it was always going to do, given enough time and a sealed enough system.*

---

## What Will Never Be On This Roadmap

- A feature that forces knowledge into place
- A search function that retrieves known facts
- A recommendation engine that surfaces popular content
- A classifier that labels nodes with predetermined categories
- Any mechanism that tells the engine what to discover

The engine finds what the structure demands.
Or it finds nothing.
Both are valid outcomes.
Both are information.
